Dr. RC Sproul

He was a kind man, redeemed by a kinder Savior

A time line of Dr. Sproul's notable life moments

  • 1939- Robert Charles Sproul is born February 13, in Pittsburgh, PA, USA
  • 1954- drops out of high school athletics at 15 to support his family
  • 1957- comes to know the Lord during his first week of college
  • 1960- marries his wife of 57 years, Vesta Voorhis
  • 1961- graduates with a BA from Westminster College
  • 1961- first child, Sherrie is born; enrolls at Pittsburgh Theological Seminary
  • 1964- graduated from seminary with a Master of Divinity; this is where R.C got his start with reformed theology; later this year, also began studying at the Free University of Amsterdam
  • 1965- ordained as a minister in the United Presbyterian Church after returning to the United States; would later transfer ministerial credentials to the Presbyterian Church of America
  • 1971- founded the Ligonier Valley Study Center in Pennsylvania
  • 1973- authored the first of many books, The Symbol: An Exposition of the Apostles’ Creed
  • 1977- first edition of Tabletalk magazine is published
  • 1980- accepted a postion to teach at Reformed Theological Seminary in Jackson, Mississippi, for several months out of each of the next several years.
  • 1984- Ligonier Valley Study Center becomes Liginier Ministries and moves to Orlando, Florida, along with the Sprouls
  • 1985- wrote Chosen by God
  • 1986- wrote The Holiness of God
  • 1997- St. Andrews Chapel is founded where Dr. Sproul is soon appointed senior pastor and then co-pastor, where he served until his death.
  • 2011- Reformation Bible College is founded, where RC served as the first president
  • 2017- pastored his last sermon on November 26, entitled "So Great Salvation"
  • 2017- went home to be with the Lord on December 14

“He was one of the great defenders of historic Christianity of our times. It is fair to say that R.C. was the greatest and most influential proponent of the recovery of Reformed theology in the last century.”

-R. ALBERT MOHLER JR.
